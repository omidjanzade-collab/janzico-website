Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Agricultural Excellence

JANZI is a premier agricultural export company headquartered in South Khorasan — the heartland of Iran's legendary saffron production. We bridge the gap between Iran's fertile harvests and discerning buyers across Russia, Central Asia, the Gulf, and Europe.

From harvest to your warehouse, every step of our supply chain is governed by rigorous quality protocols, international certifications, and a genuine commitment to long-term partnership.
